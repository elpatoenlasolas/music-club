A bit of HISTORY...

The foundations of this club go back to the year 2011, when the founders, went travelling through South America.

They were on a journey of music and surfing, and they enjoyed plenty of jam sessions from Buenos Aires all the way to the Ecuatorian beaches.

After arriving back to their home place in Argentina, they decided to start a music club that would encourage diversity, creating and unique jam session club that all the musicians in the scene would like to be part of.

The Club is located in the city of Buenos Aires, surrounded by the most popular entertainment venues and theaters.

It has a fully equipped PA, instruments and a Steinway piano for jazz nights. There is a lobby with a canteen, book club, and various offices used during daytime events. Club members have advantages over those who just visit at night, so I recommend you visit the enrollment section and become a member of our exclusive and wonderful club.

How to enrol?

To enrol, the applicants for membership should send a letter to our main office with the subject 'Membership Application'.

Here they should send a formal request with personal information, stating why they want to join the club, which ways they can contribute to the benefit of our community and showing proof that they can fund at least 6 months of membership fee.

The address of our main office is Av. 9 de Julio 1558, dpto 7 A. We will review the application and get back ASAP - the minimum time for reviewing is 2 weeks.
